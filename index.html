About The Common Crow:

The object of this website is to shed light on important social and political issues, as well as on the failures of our current two party system.

My goal is to illustrate the value, and validity of socialism, and why you should be in support of its rise.

Because, I believe that we as human beings all want the same thing...

We all want to be HAPPY.

Socialism is, in my eyes, the clearest path to that happiness.
